IDENTIFY & INVEST

Experts and ordinary citizens identify data on potential asssets linked to trafficking. They send this data to ACET or invest in RECOVER.

REPORT

ACET analyzes the data. Once verified, data providers are issued toekns. ACET compiles report on trafficking assets, gives to relevent authorities and follows up.

CONVERT

ACET collaborates with authorities to channel part of seized assets into remedial projects run by RECOVER partners. Data providers and investors exchange tokens for rewards.
